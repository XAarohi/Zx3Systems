About

Researcher

Xssandra Aarohi

Independent researcher developing interconnected frameworks spanning AI safety, consciousness theory, ethical governance, and measurement formalization. Working at the intersection of formal specification and practical application.

The work is conducted solo under significant resource constraints, primarily from mobile devices — a constraint that shapes the emphasis on clarity, minimalism, and frameworks that work without institutional infrastructure.

Organization

Zx3 Systems

Zx3 Systems is an independent research organization building multi-valence frameworks for ethical governance, AI safety, and human thriving. The work produces formal specifications, practical tools, and accessible writing — all published under open licenses.

The organizational mission: develop infrastructure that makes good decisions easier than bad ones, and make that infrastructure freely available to anyone who needs it.
